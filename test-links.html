Fact Checker Source Links Test

Test Statements

China has a population of 1.4 billion people.

Stanford University was founded in 1885.

The United States has the world's largest economy.

Scientists discovered that the Earth orbits the Sun.

Expected Behavior

Hover Behavior:

  • Hover over highlighted text to see tooltips
  • Tooltips stay visible for 2 seconds after leaving the text
  • Tooltips remain open when hovering over them
  • Source links are clickable and open in new tabs

Source Links:

  • Wikipedia sources link to specific articles
  • World Bank sources link to data pages
  • Other sources link to their respective websites
  • Links have hover effects and visual feedback

Visual Indicators:

  • Source links are blue and more prominent than badges
  • Links have hover animations (lift effect)
  • Tooltip shows "(click to verify)" hint
  • Smooth animations for tooltip appearance
